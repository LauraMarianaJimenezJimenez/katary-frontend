Se debe respetar la capacidad máxima indicada en el anuncio y la reserva.

Visitantes: solo ingresan si están pre-registrados y se registran con documento original.

No se permite el ingreso de menores de edad sin la compañía de sus padres o representante legal, quienes deberán registrarse igualmente con documento original.

Respete ascensores, pasillos, recepción, parqueaderos y demás zonas comunes.

No deje basura, colillas, botellas o elementos personales en áreas comunes.

No se permite fumar en zonas no autorizadas ni alterar sistemas de seguridad (cámaras, detectores, cerraduras, etc.).

Responda por el buen uso de muebles, paredes, lencería, electrodomésticos y dotación.

Está prohibido mover mobiliario de manera que dañe pisos/paredes o generar riesgos.

La basura debe dejarse embolsada y en el lugar indicado.

Queda prohibido: ingresar o permitir el ingreso de personas sin registro y sin documento original; exceder la capacidad del apartamento; fiestas/escándalos; consumo/venta de sustancias ilegales; portar armas o elementos peligrosos; daños intencionales o por negligencia; actividades de explotación sexual, trata o similares.

Guest House Rules

Short-term Rentals – Katary Building

These rules apply to all guests staying in, or entering as visitors to, the short-term rental apartments in the Katary Building. Acceptance of these rules is a condition of the booking and of entry into the building.

1) Purpose

To ensure a safe, quiet, and respectful stay for guests, neighbors, building staff, and service providers.

2) Mandatory entry registration (NON-NEGOTIABLE REQUIREMENT)

To protect everyone and comply with security protocols:

  1. Every person entering the building must register at the front desk/security.
  2. An ORIGINAL, valid ID must be shown (photos, copies, or screenshots are not accepted): national ID, foreign ID, passport, or another valid document as applicable.
  3. The following will be recorded: full name, ID number, entry/exit date and time, and the apartment being visited.
  4. Unregistered persons are not allowed to enter.
  5. Guests must provide (when requested) the list of occupants in advance to speed up check-in; however, security will still require the original ID.

Failure to comply may result in denied entry and/or a fine (see fines table).

3) Capacity, occupancy, and visitors

The maximum occupancy stated in the listing and reservation must be respected.

Visitors are allowed only if pre-registered and registered upon entry with an original ID.

Handing over keys, cards, or access credentials to unauthorized third parties is prohibited.

4) Minors

Minors are not allowed to enter without their parents or legal guardian, who must also register with an original ID.

If a situation suggests a minor's rights may be at risk, the competent authorities will be notified immediately.

5) Zero tolerance: sexual exploitation and trafficking

At Katary we oppose child prostitution and sex tourism.

Any act related to the sexual exploitation of children and adolescents, human trafficking, or any activity that violates rights is strictly prohibited. If there is any suspicion or evidence, the stay will be terminated and authorities will be notified, without prejudice to charges and penalties.

6) Noise, parties, and community living

Quiet hours: 8:00 p.m. to 8:00 a.m. (avoid loud music, shouting, and banging; keep volume moderate at all times).

Parties, events, and gatherings that generate complaints, excessive noise, or mass entry of people are prohibited.

Repeated complaints or intervention by security/management will result in fines.

7) Common areas and building rules

Respect elevators, hallways, reception area, parking, and all common areas.

Do not leave trash, cigarette butts, bottles, or personal items in common areas.

Smoking is not allowed in unauthorized areas, and tampering with security systems (cameras, detectors, locks, etc.) is prohibited.

8) Care of the apartment

Guests are responsible for proper use of furniture, walls, linens, appliances, and all amenities.

Moving furniture in a way that may damage floors/walls or create risks is prohibited.

Trash must be bagged and left in the designated place.

9) Main prohibitions

It is prohibited to: enter or allow entry to anyone without registration and original ID; exceed maximum occupancy; hold parties or create disturbances; use/sell illegal substances; carry weapons or dangerous items; cause intentional or negligent damage; engage in sexual exploitation, trafficking, or related activities.

10) Fines and charges for non-compliance
